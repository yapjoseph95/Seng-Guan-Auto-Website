Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for loan application?

For a standard loan application, you'll need to provide:

  • Copy of NRIC (front and back)
  • Driving license
  • Latest 3 months' salary slips
  • Latest 3 months' bank statements
  • Latest EPF statement or income tax return
  • Employment confirmation letter (if required)

Additional documents may be required depending on your employment status and the bank's requirements.

How long does the loan approval process take?

The loan approval process typically takes 1-3 working days, depending on the bank and the completeness of your documentation. In some cases, approvals can be obtained on the same day.

What is the minimum down payment required?

For most used vehicles, banks typically require a minimum down payment of 10% of the car's value. However, this can vary based on the vehicle's age, your credit profile, and the bank's policies. Our in-house financing options may offer more flexible down payment terms.

Can I get a loan with a less-than-perfect credit score?

Yes, we work with various financial institutions and have in-house financing options that cater to different credit profiles. Our finance specialists will help find a solution that works for your specific situation.

Can I refinance my existing car loan?

Yes, we offer refinancing options that may help you secure a better interest rate or more favorable terms. Our team can assess your current loan and advise on potential refinancing benefits.
