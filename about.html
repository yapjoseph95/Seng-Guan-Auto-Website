About Us

Learn more about Seng Guan Auto and our commitment to quality

Our Story

Established in 1995, Seng Guan Auto Sdn. Bhd. has grown from a small family business to one of Malaysia's most trusted used car dealerships. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide quality pre-owned vehicles at fair prices with exceptional customer service.

For over 25 years, we have maintained our commitment to transparency, integrity, and customer satisfaction. What sets us apart is our dedication to thoroughly inspecting and verifying every vehicle we sell, ensuring our customers drive away with confidence.

Today, under the leadership of our founder Mr. Tan Seng Guan and his sons, we continue to uphold the values that have made us successful: honesty, quality, and putting customers first.
