Our Story

1995 Founded
30+ Years Experience
3000+ Happy Customers

Established in 1995, Seng Guan Auto has grown from a small family business to one of Malaysia's most trusted used car dealerships. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide quality pre-owned vehicles at fair prices with exceptional customer service.

For over 30 years, we have maintained our commitment to transparency, integrity, and customer satisfaction. What sets us apart is our dedication to thoroughly inspecting and verifying every vehicle we sell, ensuring our customers drive away with confidence.

Today, under the leadership of our founder Mr. Ang Kin Guan and his sons, we continue to uphold the values that have made us successful: honesty, quality, and putting customers first.
